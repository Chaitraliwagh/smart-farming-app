Sample Images for Testing
==========================

For testing the disease detection feature, you can use these types of images:

1. Healthy Leaf Images:
   - Take photos of healthy green leaves
   - Ensure good lighting and clear focus
   - Image should be primarily green

2. Diseased Leaf Images:
   - Photos showing brown spots (leaf spot, blight)
   - Yellow or brown discoloration (rust)
   - White powdery coating (powdery mildew)

You can download test images from:
- PlantVillage Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/emmarex/plantdisease
- Or use your own plant photos

Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: Any (will be resized to 224x224)
- Max file size: 5MB

Place your test images in this directory for easy access.
